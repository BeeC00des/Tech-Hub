Akure Tech Hub

The Akure Tech Hub features a mix of an open co-working community space accessible through membership and an incubation program. Founded as a unique tripartite coalition between the Ondo State Government, Akure TechUp and the Federal University of Technology, Akure (FUTA), the hub is tasked with the vision of nurturing the existing ecosystem and making Akure the proposed technological investment destination in West Africa.
